# Hallelujah! Hallelujah!

Hallelujah! Hallelujah! In his temple God be praised; 
in the high and heav'nly places be the sounding anthem raised. 
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Amen.

-----

Hallelujah! Praise Jehovah for his mighty acts of fame; 
excellent his might and greatness, fitting praises then proclaim. 
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Amen.

-----

Hallelujah! Praise Jehovah with the trumpet's joyful sound; 
praise with harp and praise with organ, let his glorious praise abound. 
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Amen.

-----

Hallelujah! Hallelujah! All that breathe, Jehovah praise; 
let the voices God has given joyful anthems to him raise. 
Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Hallelujah! Amen.

-----

let the voices God has given joyful anthems to him raise.
