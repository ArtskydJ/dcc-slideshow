# My Tribute (Psalm 115)

How can I say thanks for the things you have done for me?
Things so undeserved. Yet you gave to prove
your love for me; the voices of a million 
Angles could not express my gratitude.

-----

All that I am. And ever hope to be, I owe is all to thee. To God be the glory, to God be the
Glory, to God be the glory for the things he has done. 
With his blood he has saved me; with his pow'r he has raised me;

-----

To God be the glory for the things he has done. Just let me live my life let it be pleasing Lord,
To thee; and if I gain any praise, lit it go to Calvary. With his
